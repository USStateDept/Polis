Polis is...

A tool designed to better make sense of how subnational politicians and policymankers view and prioritize issues across four thematic areas outlined in the 2015 QDDR report:

CVE Combatting violent extremism

IEG Promoting inclusive economic growth

MACC Mitigating and adapting to climate change

RODS Creating resiliant and open democratic societies

Specifically, it is a prototype web application that collects and analyzes public statements by and about sub-national political public figures (i.e., mayors and governors) in order to better understand how - and on what issues - to engage with them.
